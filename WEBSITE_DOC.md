## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### Q: What is a Performance Bond?
A: A performance bond is a surety bond issued by an insurance company or a bank to guarantee satisfactory completion of a project by a contractor. It protects project owners from financial loss if the contractor fails to fulfill the terms and conditions of a contract.

### Q: How long does it take to get a bond?
A: Most applications are processed within 24 to 48 hours depending on the complexity and documentation provided. Complex applications may take longer.

### Q: What information is required?
A: Usually, we need your company financial statements, personal indemnity, and specific project details to start the underwriting process.

### Q: What types of bonds do you offer?
A: We offer Performance Bonds, Payment Bonds, Bid Bonds, Maintenance Bonds, and other custom bonding solutions tailored to your needs.

### Q: Do you offer financing?
A: Yes, we offer premium financing with flexible payment options to help manage your business cash flow effectively.
